Cheerleading

    The captain and co-captain work together to communicate with the coach and the team about practice schedules, rotation, notices, and expectations. Most importantly, the captain and co-captain work together to choreograph a routine a competition called KAIAC, Korean-American Interscholastic Activities Conference. In the routine, we evaluate each member’s skill for dance, tumbling, jump, then put them in different formations. Then, we teach and constantly give individual feedback on this piece to improve the overall skill and synchronization.
